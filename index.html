Beaches are in Danger

Climate change is affecting more than just the polar bears, it affects our daily lives. The trend of global warming has created a looming threat upon our beaches that has been increasing over past decades, mainly due to preventable human activities.

  • Get Started

The Cause

An average Americans pumps out 17 tonns of Carbon Dioxide per year. Multiply this number by millions and it is clear to see the devastation humans can cause. This carbon, methane, and other emmissions society brings about does not just disappear. Instead it ends up everywhere even our most beloved places, such as beaches.

Rising Tides

Over 97 % of scientists agree that the climate is changing, why can't we? The beaches are dying, the rising tides eliminate potential safe areas, while increasing frequency of storms brings about a much more dangerous ocean. When there is so much on the line why can't society come to a consensus.

The Good Memories

Climate Change will do more than just alter the beaches, the fun getaway place, it will also alter the vision you have of beaches. No longer will the beache be the nice and inviting place, there will more danger associated with it. These fun times people share at the beach, those memories where fun and adventure were mixed, will start to disappear.

What could you do?

Now that you know the risks, see what you could do in order to Bend the Curve?

Go Solar

Solar could help reduce the coal resources used in creating energy and cause the climate to keep in check, so the tides don't rise.

Eat less meat

Try to diversify your diet in order to decrease the ammount of livestock contributing to the heating. Eat fresh foods offered near the beach.

Public transport

Using a bus or other methods of mass transit to get to the beach, will help contribute to well being of the beach.

Know your trash bins

Putting the trash you have in the recycle bin will both help the wonderful beach filled planet, and will keep the beach clean.

Spread the Knowledge

Letting others know about this issue also causes public officals to take a serious tone with matters concerning you!

Start Voting

Vote in local elections for changes that are direct to your lifestyle, such as the upkeep of beaches.
